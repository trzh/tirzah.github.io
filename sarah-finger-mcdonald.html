Sarah Finger McDonald for OR-16

Graphic designer

From 03/2024 to 11/2024

In my role as a graphic designer at Winning Mark, a media firm based out of Portland, Oregon, I worked on campaign social graphics, ads, and videos for Sarah Finger McDonald, a gun safety advocate running for State Representative. Using her established fonts and a bold palette of purples and greens, I designed visuals that underscored her dedication to creating safer communities. My work focused on highlighting her values and policies, crafting messaging that was clear, approachable, and deeply aligned with her commitment to gun safety and public well-being. By balancing strong visuals with thoughtful design, I helped amplify her voice and connect with her audience.
