Love in a Jar is a concept for a simple mobile app that shows positive notes to users. They submit their own notes—which can be positive affirmations, instructions for activities that the user enjoys, or anything else the user finds helpful—into a metaphorical jar and the app randomly shuffles them and returns them to the user. They can also opt to receive messages from friends, and are able to send notes to their friends' jars as well. Notes they receive from friends are added into their jar and are randomly returned to the user.
