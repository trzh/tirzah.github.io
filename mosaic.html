I designed marketing materials for UMBC Campus Life's Mosaic.

series event flyers

SKILLS: BRANDING & IDENTITY, TYPOGRAPHY

TOOLS: ADOBE PHOTOSHOP, ADOBE INDESIGN

I design flyers for on campus and virtual events hosted by mosaic. I strive to make each one unique, engaging & eye-catching. We receive assignments on a weekly basis—one design a week, and we typically have about four days to turn in a design to get feedback. After that, we have 24 hours to make any required changes. Because we receive assignments on a weekly basis, I always push myself to try new styles and ambitious designs, but each flyer is recognizably from mosaic, because of the consistency in the logo and the border across the bottom.
