Analyst News

Art director

From 01/2023 to now

Analyst News is a nonprofit, entirely volunteer-run news organization dedicated to thoughtful journalism and critical analysis that prioritizes absolute justice above all else. As the art director, my role encompasses selecting photos, creating original editorial illustrations, and developing branding guides and templates for social media graphics and videos. I manage design workflows and approve all graphics and story art before publishing. During a recent organizational relaunch, I successfully led our globally-distributed design team, managing deadlines and collaborating across multiple teams and time zones.
