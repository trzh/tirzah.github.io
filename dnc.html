Democratic National Committee

Graphic designer

From 06/2021 to 11/2022

As a part of the Mobilization team, I designed ads, emails, merch & social graphics that reached millions of people and raised hundreds of millions of dollars to elect Democrats up and down the ballot during the 2022 midterm elections. I pioneered new social media and email templates to deliver information in fresh and effective ways. My designs have appeared on President Joe Biden's Instagram and gotten signed by him, and they've even gotten roasted on FOX News and other conservative outlets.

I designed a bus wrap proposal for a nationwide bus tour for Democratic officials. The goals of the tour were not only to help get out the vote ahead of the 2022 midterm elections, but also to celebrate where the Democratic Party is as well as get people excited about the work still lying ahead.

One proposed design is more traditional but still celebratory, representing what the party is and what it has accomplished.

The other design is more exciting and youthful, representing, to me, what the party could be. Symbols like the road and the ballot box call upon the work that the Democratic Party still must do.
