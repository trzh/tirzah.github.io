i design flyers at the UMBC student events board every week.

every week, we're given flyer assignments for events that are coming up. we have four days to design, and then 24 hours to make any edits our team lead gives us. my goal with these flyers is to make them as eye-catching as possible. our target audience is the UMBC student body, who we aim to serve and engage.

i design social media posts at hackUMBC.

in the weeks leading up to hackUMBC, the design team works with the marketing team to develop a strategy for posts and social media engagement. we also work with the website team to design assets for the website based on the theme of the year. our fall 2019 theme was space.
