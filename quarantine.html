quarantine PSAs.

after hearing a lot of dialogue about productivity culture during quarantine from friends, family and industry professionals alike, i decided to channel my frustration into something more, well, productive. i used a wacom intuos tablet to make these illustrations in photoshop after creating a few pen & paper sketches. i posted all of these on instagram as a way to spread more positive messages to people struggling with mental health during a very difficult time.
