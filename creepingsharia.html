Creeping Sharia is a monthly newsletter with over 1000 subscribers that highlights journalism about Muslims in America. I was inspired by Islamic mosaic tile patterns when designing this set of graphics.

I designed this banner for the top of each edition based on the requirements of Substack, the mailing list service used by Creeping Sharia.

This banner is for a special edition of Creeping Sharia, in partnership with the Institute of Social Policy and Understanding.
